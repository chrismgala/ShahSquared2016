Our Story

It all started on the 4th of July...

For years, one of Harsh's best friends, Jinesh, had asked Harsh to visit him in LA. 7 years later, in 2013, Harsh finally decided to make the trip. That weekend, Jinesh was invited to a family get-together to celebrate the 4th of July so he decided to bring Harsh along (with no intention of setting him up!). Most of Jinesh's cousins had already arrived, including Ami. As soon as Harsh walked through the door, Ami got very giggly and nervous (she would've been blushing if she weren't brown!) After a night of food, games, fireworks, and of course playful flirting, Harsh reached out to Ami to see if there was more to the spark (spoiler alert: there is!)

After 6 months of long distance dating, Harsh eventually made the big move... to LA! It was at that point Harsh and Ami knew they wanted to spend the rest of their lives together. Since that time, they enjoyed getting to know each other better and have truly become best friends.

In October 2015, Ami was upset she thought Harsh had forgotten her birthday as he didn't plan anything for the big day. The day after Ami's birthday, Harsh surprised her with a picnic at their favorite park overlooking the LA skyline. It was that day Harsh got down on one knee and asked Ami to spend the rest of her life with him. She said yes! He later brought her to tears by hosting a surprise party at her favorite restaurant with their family and friends.

This is just the beginning of our story. We have many more laughs to be had, memories to be made, and chapters to be written. We look forward to sharing this next step in our journey with our beloved family and friends. See you all in November!
